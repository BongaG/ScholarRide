Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Scholar-Ride is a campus transport management system developed exclusively for the Durban University of Technology (DUT) community. We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

2. Information We Collect

When you register and use Scholar-Ride we collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • DUT email address
  • Phone number
  • Student or staff number
  • Faculty or department
  • Ride history and booking information
  • Location data related to ride origins and destinations
3. How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity as a DUT student, staff member, or driver
  • To facilitate ride bookings and transport management
  • To send notifications about your rides and bookings
  • To improve the Scholar-Ride platform
  • To ensure the safety and accountability of all users
4. How We Protect Your Information

Scholar-Ride takes the security of your personal information seriously. We implement the following measures:

  • All passwords are encrypted using industry standard hashing
  • Student and staff numbers are only visible to authorised users
  • Registration plate numbers are only visible to administrators and drivers
  • Access to personal data is restricted based on user roles
5. Sharing of Information

Scholar-Ride does not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your information is only accessible to authorised DUT administrators and the Scholar-Ride system.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information held by Scholar-Ride
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa
7. Data Retention

Your personal information is retained for as long as your account is active on Scholar-Ride. If you request deletion of your account all personal information associated with your account will be permanently removed from our system.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your personal information is handled please contact the Scholar-Ride administrator through the platform.
