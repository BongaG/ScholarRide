Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Scholar-Ride is a campus transport management system developed exclusively for the Durban University of Technology (DUT) community. We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with POPIA.

2. Information We Collect

We collect your full name, DUT email, phone number, student or staff number, faculty, ride history and booking information.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to verify your DUT identity, facilitate ride bookings, send notifications, and ensure safety and accountability on the platform.

4. How We Protect Your Information

All passwords are encrypted. Student numbers and registration plates are only visible to authorised users. Access is restricted based on user roles.

5. Sharing of Information

Scholar-Ride does not sell or share your personal information with any third parties.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. You may also lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

7. Data Retention

Your information is retained for as long as your account is active. Deleting your account removes all associated personal data permanently.

8. Contact

For any questions about this policy contact the Scholar-Ride administrator through the platform.
